Coronavirus and Sports

Professional Sports

Sports accross the world are being drastically affected. One player on the Utah Jazz, center Rudy Gobert, was recently diagnosed with coronavirus and as a result the NBA has suspended their season.

• Major League Baseball has closed their locker rooms to reporters in preseason and some regular season games will be moved to more remote stadiums from the coronavirus outbreak areas.

• In soccar, Juventus player Daniele Rugani has tested positive for coronavirus. La Liga has suspended at least two days of matches.

• In Miami, a NASCAR race on March 22 has been postponed.

Amatuer Sports

College athletics are also being affected. The NCAA has just cancelled the remainder of all winter and spring championships, including March Madness.

• Ivy league has canceled the remainder of the mens and womens basketball seasons. NESCAC has gpne further and cancelled all spring sports, including womens softball.

• The 2020 Summer Olymics in Tokyo has announced that coronavirus may cause problems with events.

• College football powerhouses Michigan and Ohio State have cancelled spring games.
